1. Introduction

Welcome to YuanqiZhiKe Toolkit ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you visit and use our website and tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with GDPR, CCPA, and other global privacy regulations.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

YuanqiZhiKe Toolkit is designed with privacy as a core principle. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • We do not require user registration or login.
  • We do not store any data you input into our tools on our servers.
  • All tool processing happens locally on your device -- no data is transmitted to any remote server.

3. Anonymous Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics 4 or Cloudflare Analytics) to collect anonymous, aggregated website usage statistics. This data includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring websites and search terms
  • General geographic region (country-level only)
  • Browser type and operating system (non-specific)

This information cannot be used to identify you personally. We do not combine analytics data with any personal information.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality (session management, preference storage). These do not require consent.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used by Google Analytics 4 / Cloudflare Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. IP addresses are anonymized.
  • Advertising Cookies: We display Google AdSense advertisements across our website pages. Google AdSense may deploy advertising cookies to deliver personalized advertisements according to visitor browsing behaviors. Visitors can disable personalized advertising by accessing Google official ad privacy settings. All ad-related data collection follows Google privacy rules and global data compliance regulations.

For detailed cookie categories and management options, please see our Cookie Notice.
