About Us

Empowering creators worldwide with free, privacy-first tools.

Our Mission

YuanqiZhiKe Toolkit was founded with a simple belief: everyone deserves access to powerful, professional-grade tools -- regardless of their location, income, or technical background. We build free, offline-capable frontend tools that respect your privacy and put you in control of your data.

What Makes Us Different

🔒 Privacy First

Every tool runs entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device. No servers, no tracking, no compromises.

💰 Completely Free

No sign-ups, no paywalls, no hidden fees. All tools are free to use and free to download with full source code.

🌍 Global Community

Our tools are designed for creators worldwide -- from independent developers to enterprise teams. Multilingual support ensures accessibility for everyone.

📦 Open Source

All tools come with full source code under a permissive license. Study, modify, and learn from production-ready code.

Our Story

YuanqiZhiKe started as a small project to help Chinese creators access the same quality of tools that Western developers take for granted. Over time, we expanded our catalog to serve a global audience, growing from a handful of tools to a comprehensive toolkit covering content writing, design, development, business, and education.

Today, our toolkit serves thousands of users daily across dozens of countries. We remain committed to our founding principles: free, private, and open.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: Tools should be available to everyone, everywhere
  • Privacy: Your data belongs to you, and stays on your device
  • Transparency: Full source code, clear licensing, honest communication
  • Quality: Production-ready code that works reliably in real-world scenarios
  • Community: Building together, sharing knowledge, empowering creators
