How to Use

  1. Choose jewelry type (ring, bracelet, necklace, bangle).
  2. Select measurement method (circumference, diameter, or US size).
  3. Enter your measurement value.
  4. Adjust for knuckle size if needed.
  5. Click Calculate Size for international size conversion.

Measure at end of day when fingers are largest. Use paper strip method: wrap paper around finger, mark overlap, measure with ruler. Convert mm to inches by dividing by 25.4.
