How to Use

  1. Enter your vehicle weight (check owner manual or weigh station).
  2. Find max tire load on tire sidewall (e.g. LOAD INDEX 1477 lbs).
  3. Enter max tire pressure from sidewall (e.g. MAX 44 PSI).
  4. Set number of tires (4 for car, 6 for dually truck).
  5. Enter current temperature for adjustment.
  6. Choose load condition (empty, normal, or full).
  7. Enter door sticker PSI for comparison.
  8. Click Calculate Tire Pressure for recommendation.

Tire pressure changes with temperature (~1% per 10°F). Always check cold. Door sticker is for normal load; increase 3-6 PSI for heavy loads. Under-inflation is the #1 cause of tire failure.
