How to Use

  1. Enter number of people.
  2. Choose comfort level.
  3. Set average height and gear storage.
  4. Click Calculate.

Rule: buy tent rated 1-2 people larger than group. Space: 15 sq ft/person (cramped), 25 (comfortable), 40 (luxury). Add 10 sq ft/person for gear inside. Peak height: standing needs ~190cm, kneeling ~110cm, sleeping ~90cm. Vestibule stores gear outside living area.
