How to Use

  1. Enter property price and monthly rent.
  2. Add annual expenses (tax, insurance, etc.).
  3. Set financing details.
  4. Click Calculate Yield.

Gross yield = annual rent / price. Net yield = NOI / price (after expenses). Cap rate = NOI / price. Cash on cash = annual cash flow / down payment. Rules: 1% (monthly rent = 1% price), 50% (expenses = 50% rent). Good: gross > 8%, net > 5%, CoC > 8%.
