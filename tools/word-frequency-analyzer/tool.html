How to Use

  1. Paste your text in the Text area.
  2. Toggle options: case sensitivity, stop words, numbers, min length.
  3. See top 20 words by frequency with counts and percentages.
  4. View a word cloud showing relative word sizes.

Lexical diversity = unique words / total words. Higher diversity = more varied vocabulary (good for writing). For SEO, aim for 1-3% keyword density on target keywords. Stop words (the, is, a) are usually filtered out as they don't carry meaning.
