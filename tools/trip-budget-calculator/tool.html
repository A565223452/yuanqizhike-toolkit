How to Use

  1. Enter destination, trip duration, and number of travelers.
  2. Fill in one-time costs (flights, lodging per night, insurance).
  3. Set daily costs per person (food, activities, transport).
  4. Add shopping budget and buffer percentage.
  5. Click Calculate to see total, per-person, and per-day costs.

Budget ranges: Budget travel $50-80/day, Mid-range $100-200/day, Luxury $300+/day. Accommodation is typically 30-40% of total budget.
