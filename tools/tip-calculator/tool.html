How to Use

  1. Enter the bill amount.
  2. Choose a tip percentage (15-20% standard in US).
  3. Set number of people to split the bill.
  4. Optional: round up or tip on pre-tax amount.

US tipping customs: 15-20% at sit-down restaurants, $1-2 per drink at bars, 10-15% for delivery. Tipping norms vary by country — research local customs when traveling.
