How to Use

  1. Fill required fields (marked with *): headline, author, image, date, publisher, URL.
  2. Choose the article type (Article, NewsArticle, BlogPosting).
  3. Add optional fields (section, word count, language, body excerpt).
  4. Copy the generated JSON-LD script tag into your page's <head>.
  5. Test with Google Rich Results Test before deploying.

Schema.org Article markup enables rich snippets in Google Search. Required fields per Google guidelines: headline, image, datePublished, author, publisher.
