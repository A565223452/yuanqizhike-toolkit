How to Use

  1. Enter your current odometer reading and the miles at your last oil change.
  2. Select oil type — synthetic oils last longer than conventional.
  3. Choose driving conditions: severe conditions (short trips, extreme heat/cold, dusty roads, towing) require more frequent changes.
  4. Enter your annual mileage to estimate when the next change is due in time.
  5. Click Calculate for a full maintenance schedule.

General guidelines: Conventional oil every 3,000–5,000 mi. Synthetic blend every 5,000–7,500 mi. Full synthetic every 7,500–10,000 mi. Severe conditions halve these intervals. Always follow your vehicle manufacturer's recommendations.
