How to Use

  1. Select Grade level (elementary, middle, or high school).
  2. Choose Category (biology, chemistry, physics, earth, environmental, psychology, technology).
  3. Set Budget limits and number of ideas to generate.
  4. Click Generate ideas for project suggestions with hypothesis and method.

Each idea includes a hypothesis to test, experimental method, materials list, time estimate, and difficulty rating. Always verify safety with a teacher and follow lab protocols.
