How to Use

  1. Paste your text in the textarea.
  2. Click Check Grammar.
  3. Review highlighted issues with explanations.

Detects: common typos (teh, recieve), article errors (a apple), double spaces, capitalization, your/you are, its/it is, their/there confusion. For advanced checks use Grammarly or LanguageTool.
