How to Use

  1. Fill in company info.
  2. Select sections to include.
  3. Choose length (short / standard / detailed).
  4. Click Generate to preview, Print to save as PDF.

Standard business plan covers 10-12 sections. Investors (VCs/angels) expect: market size, competitive moat, financial projections, team, and funding ask. Keep Executive Summary concise (1-2 pages). Update regularly — a stale plan signals lack of execution.
