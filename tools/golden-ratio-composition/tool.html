How to Use

  1. Enter image dimensions.
  2. See rule of thirds and golden ratio lines.
  3. Get power points for subject placement.
  4. Check golden rectangle recommendations.

Golden ratio φ = 1.618. Rule of thirds (33%) approximates golden ratio (38.2%/61.8%). Place subjects on power points (intersections). Leading lines guide viewer's eye. Golden spiral (Fibonacci) is aesthetically pleasing. Common golden ratios: 16:10, 1:1.618.
