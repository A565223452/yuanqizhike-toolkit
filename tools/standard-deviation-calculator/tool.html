How to Use

  1. Enter numbers separated by commas or spaces.
  2. Choose Sample (n-1, for sample data) or Population (N).
  3. Click Calculate for full statistics.

Use Sample when data is a sample of a larger population. Use Population when data IS the entire population. Standard deviation measures data spread around the mean.
