How to Use

  1. Enter meeting title, date, time, duration.
  2. List attendees and location/meeting link.
  3. Add agenda items, decisions, and action items with owners and due dates.
  4. Choose output format: Markdown, plain text, or HTML.
  5. Copy the notes to your wiki or document.

Markdown format works well with Notion, GitHub, GitLab, Confluence. Action items with clear owners and due dates improve accountability.
