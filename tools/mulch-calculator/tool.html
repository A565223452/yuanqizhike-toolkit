How to Use

  1. Enter each garden bed with name and dimensions in feet.
  2. Set desired mulch depth (2-3 inches typical, 4 inches for new beds).
  3. Choose bag size and enter price per bag.
  4. Click Calculate for cubic yards, bags, and cost.

Bulk mulch sold by cubic yard (27 cu ft). Bagged mulch typically 2 cu ft. One cubic yard covers ~108 sq ft at 3" depth.
