How to Use

  1. Enter amount and select unit (volume or weight).
  2. Choose ingredient for accurate gram conversions.
  3. See all volume and weight conversions.

Volumetric measurements (cups, tbsp) vary by ingredient density. 1 cup of flour (~120g) ≠ 1 cup of sugar (~200g). For consistent baking, use a kitchen scale and measure by weight (grams). US recipes use cups; EU/UK use grams. 1 US cup = 236.6 ml, while 1 metric cup = 250 ml.
