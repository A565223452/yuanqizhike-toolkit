How to Use

  1. Enter the total Flour weight for your bake.
  2. Set target Hydration % (70-80% is typical for sourdough).
  3. Adjust Salt and Levain percentages if desired.
  4. Choose your Levain hydration based on your starter type.
  5. Click Calculate Recipe for the formula, or Starter Feeding for feeding ratios.

Baker's % expresses water/salt/levain as a percentage of flour weight. Levain flour counts toward total flour, levain water toward total water - the calculator handles this automatically.
