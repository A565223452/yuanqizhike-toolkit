How to Use

  1. Select IRS mileage rate (or custom).
  2. Add trips with date, odometer readings, purpose.
  3. Click Calculate.

IRS 2024 rates: Business 67¢/mi, Medical/Moving 22¢/mi, Charity 14¢/mi. Deduction = miles × rate. Must keep detailed log: date, miles, start/end odometer, business purpose. Cannot deduct commute to regular workplace. Self-employed: file Schedule C. Employees: TCJA suspended misc deductions through 2025.
