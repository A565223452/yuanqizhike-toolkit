How to Use

  1. Enter annual miles and years of ownership.
  2. Set gas price and electricity rate.
  3. Enter efficiency: Gas MPG, Hybrid MPG, EV mi/kWh.
  4. Set EV charging mix: % home vs public.
  5. Click Compare for total cost analysis.

Average US: Gas 28 MPG, Hybrid 50 MPG, EV 3.5 mi/kWh. Home charging at $0.15/kWh is much cheaper than public DC fast charging at $0.40/kWh. EVs also save 50% on maintenance.
