How to Use

  1. Enter distance and elevation gain.
  2. Set pack weight and fitness level.
  3. Choose terrain type.
  4. Click Calculate.

Naismith's Rule: 5 km/h + 1 hour per 600m ascent. Adjustments: fitness (-30% to +60%), terrain (-30% to -50%), pack weight (-15% if >15kg). Plan 5 min breaks per hour, 30 min lunch for 4h+ hikes. Carry 0.5L water per hour. Allow buffer time.
