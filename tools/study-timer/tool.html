How to Use

  1. Set focus/break durations (defaults: 25/5/15 min).
  2. Set number of sessions per cycle (default: 4).
  3. Click Start to begin focus session.
  4. Take breaks when timer switches automatically.
  5. Track your progress in the stats panel.

Pomodoro Technique: 25 min focus + 5 min break = 1 session. After 4 sessions, take 15-30 min long break. Stay focused during work. Move during breaks. Track progress to stay motivated. Allow notifications for alerts.
