How to Use

  1. Enter latitude and longitude for two points.
  2. Or select a preset city pair.
  3. Click Calculate for distance and bearing.
  4. See flight time and driving estimates.

Uses Haversine formula - calculates great-circle distance on a sphere. Earth radius: 6,371 km. Accuracy: ~0.5%. Bearing: compass direction from A to B. Flight time assumes 900 km/h average speed. Driving estimate: 1.3x distance at 80 km/h.
