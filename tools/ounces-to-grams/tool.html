How to Use

  1. Switch between Weight (oz ↔ g) and Volume (fl oz ↔ ml) tabs.
  2. Type in either field — the other updates automatically.
  3. See all related conversions.

"Ounce" (oz) is weight (28.35g); "fluid ounce" (fl oz) is volume (29.57 ml). They are NOT interchangeable — 1 fl oz of water weighs ~1 oz, but 1 fl oz of honey weighs 1.5 oz. US and UK fluid ounces differ slightly. Use weight for baking accuracy.
