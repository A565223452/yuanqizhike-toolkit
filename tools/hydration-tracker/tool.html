How to Use

  1. Enter weight and activity to set goal.
  2. Adjust climate if applicable.
  3. Click + buttons to log water intake.
  4. Use custom button for other amounts.
  5. Track progress in the log table.

Daily water need: ~35ml/kg body weight. Add 350ml per 30 min exercise. Hot climate: +500ml. Signs of dehydration: dark urine, headache, fatigue. Pale yellow urine = good hydration. Eat water-rich foods (cucumber, watermelon). Carry a water bottle.
