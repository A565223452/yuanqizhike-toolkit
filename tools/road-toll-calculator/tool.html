How to Use

  1. Select your Vehicle type and Payment method (E-ZPass or cash).
  2. Check all toll roads on your route.
  3. Click Calculate tolls.
  4. Review itemized costs and total.

Cash / by-mail rates are typically 50-100% higher than electronic tolls. RVs and trucks pay 1.5x-3x car rates. Use TollGuru or official state toll calculators for exact routing.
