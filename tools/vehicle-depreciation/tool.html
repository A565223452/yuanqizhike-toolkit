How to Use

  1. Enter purchase price of the vehicle.
  2. Set vehicle age when purchased (0 for new).
  3. Select vehicle type (depreciation rates vary by type).
  4. Enter annual mileage (12,000 is average).
  5. Choose years to project and condition.
  6. Click Calculate Depreciation for year-by-year value loss.

Cars typically lose 15-30% in year 1, then 10-15% per year. Luxury cars and EVs depreciate fastest. Trucks and sports cars hold value better. High mileage increases depreciation by ~1% per 1,000 miles over 12k/year.
