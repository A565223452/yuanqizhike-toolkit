How to Use

  1. Select pet type (dog, cat, rabbit, etc.).
  2. Enter pet age in years and months.
  3. For dogs, select size for accurate calculation.
  4. See equivalent human age.

Dog years aren't simply ×7. AVMA formula: Year 1 = 15 human years, Year 2 = +9 years, each subsequent year varies by size (small: +4, large: +6). Small dogs live longer (15 yrs), giant breeds shorter (9 yrs). Cats: Year 1 = 15, Year 2 = 25, then +4 per year.
