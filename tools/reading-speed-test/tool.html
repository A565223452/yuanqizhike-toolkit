How to Use

  1. Choose Passage difficulty from easy to college level.
  2. Click Start reading and read the passage at your normal pace.
  3. Click Finished immediately when done.
  4. Answer the comprehension questions.
  5. Submit for your WPM score and effective reading rate.

Average adult reads 200-300 WPM. Speed readers reach 400-700 WPM. Effective WPM accounts for comprehension - if you understand 70%, your effective rate is 70% of raw WPM.
