How to Use

  1. Search for a country (or browse the list).
  2. Enter a bill amount to see suggested tip.
  3. Reference tipping customs for restaurants, taxis, hotels, bartenders.

Tipping customs vary widely. In Japan, tipping can be considered rude. In the US, servers rely on tips for income (often paid below minimum wage). When in doubt, ask locals or your hotel concierge. Always tip in local currency, not USD.
