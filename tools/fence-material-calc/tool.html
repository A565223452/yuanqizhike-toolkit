How to Use

  1. Enter Fence length in feet.
  2. Choose Fence style (privacy picket, ranch rail, or chain link).
  3. Set Post spacing (8ft typical), Picket width and gap.
  4. Enter Fence height and material costs.
  5. Click Calculate materials.

Posts typically 8ft spacing. Use 4x4 posts for fences under 6ft, 4x4 or 6x6 for taller. Check local building codes for fence height limits and property line setbacks.
