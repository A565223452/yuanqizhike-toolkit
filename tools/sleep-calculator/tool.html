How to Use

  1. Select your age group (sets recommended hours).
  2. Enter wake-up time.
  3. Adjust time to fall asleep (avg 15 min).
  4. See recommended bedtimes for optimal sleep.

Sleep needs: newborns 14-17 hr, toddlers 11-14 hr, preschoolers 10-13 hr, school-age 9-11 hr, teens 8-10 hr, adults 7-9 hr, seniors 7-8 hr. Chronic sleep loss increases risk of obesity, diabetes, heart disease, depression.
