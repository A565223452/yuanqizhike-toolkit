How to Use

  1. Enter current UV index (check weather app).
  2. Select your skin type (1-6).
  3. Choose SPF applied.
  4. Click Calculate.

UV levels: Low (0-2), Moderate (3-5), High (6-7), Very High (8-10), Extreme (11+). Safe time = MED / UV. Skin Type 1: 5 min MED, Type 6: 60 min MED. SPF extends protection (real-world ~50% of label). Vitamin D: 1/3 of MED is enough. Apply sunscreen 15-30 min before sun, reapply every 2 hours.
