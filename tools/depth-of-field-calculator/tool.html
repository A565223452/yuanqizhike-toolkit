How to Use

  1. Enter focal length, aperture, and subject distance.
  2. Select sensor size.
  3. Click Calculate DoF.

Hyperfocal distance: focus here for max DoF (half to infinity sharp). DoF distribution: ~1/3 in front, 2/3 behind subject. Shallower DoF: wider aperture (lower f), longer focal length, closer subject.
