How to Use

  1. Choose the number and size of pizzas you want to make.
  2. Select your preferred Style (Neapolitan, NY, Detroit, etc.) - hydration auto-sets.
  3. Adjust hydration, salt, yeast, and oil percentages if desired (baker's %).
  4. Set Ferment time based on your schedule.
  5. Click Calculate Recipe for gram-weight ingredients.

Baker's percentages express each ingredient as a percentage of flour weight. Higher hydration = softer, airier crust but harder to handle. Cold ferment (24-72h) develops best flavor.
