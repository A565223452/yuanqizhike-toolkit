How to Use

  1. Enter your video title.
  2. Add a brief description.
  3. Choose niche and tag count.
  4. Click Generate and copy tags.

YouTube allows up to 500 characters of tags. Use 15-30 relevant tags. Mix broad (high volume) and long-tail (specific). Include title keywords. Tags are less important than title/description for SEO now, but still help with suggested videos.
