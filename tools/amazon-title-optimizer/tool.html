Amazon Listing Title Optimizer

Optimize Amazon product listing titles for better SEO. Check character limits, keyword placement, and readability. Free online tool for sellers.
Amazon Title Best Practices:
  • Keep between 150-200 characters for best visibility
  • Start with brand name and product name
  • Include key features and benefits
  • Use relevant keywords naturally
  • Avoid ALL CAPS (except brand name)
  • Don't use promotional phrases ("Best Seller", "Free Shipping")

How to Use

  1. Paste your Amazon product title into the textarea
  2. Click Analyze Title to check for issues and get suggestions
  3. Click Auto-Optimize to automatically fix common problems
  4. Copy the optimized title and paste it into your Amazon Seller Central

What gets checked

  • Character count (max 200 for Amazon)
  • ALL CAPS usage
  • Promotional phrases (prohibited by Amazon)
  • Special characters
  • Repeated keywords (keyword stuffing)
  • Brand name as first word
