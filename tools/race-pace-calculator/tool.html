How to Use

  1. Choose calculation mode (pace, time, or distance).
  2. Select race distance (5K, 10K, half, full marathon).
  3. Enter time or pace depending on mode.
  4. Click Calculate.

Pace = time ÷ distance. Training paces: Easy = +25% of race pace, Threshold = -5%, Interval = -12%. Most runs (80%) should be at easy pace. Build mileage gradually (+10%/week). Taper 1-3 weeks before race. Fuel: 30-60g carbs/hour for runs >90 min.
