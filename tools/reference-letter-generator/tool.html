How to Use

  1. Enter your details (writer name, title, organization, email).
  2. Fill in candidate name and your relationship.
  3. Specify how long you\'ve known them and in what capacity.
  4. List their key strengths (comma separated).
  5. Describe a specific achievement with details.
  6. Choose recommendation strength.
  7. Click Generate then copy or download.

Only write references you can stand behind. Use specific examples and quantifiable achievements. Compare candidate to peers when possible. Keep to one page.
