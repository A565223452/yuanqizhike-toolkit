How to Use

  1. Set your Exam date.
  2. Enter Hours/day available for studying.
  3. Add subjects with priority (Low/Medium/High) and total hours needed.
  4. Click Generate schedule.
  5. Review allocated hours per subject and a sample 7-day rotation plan.

Higher-priority subjects get more time. The tool warns if your needed hours exceed available time. Adjust hours/day or scope as needed.
