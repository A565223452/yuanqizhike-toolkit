How to Use

  1. Choose voting system: popular vote, electoral college, or parliamentary.
  2. Enter candidate names and vote counts.
  3. For electoral/parliament: also enter electoral votes or seats.
  4. Click Calculate for results, winner, and margin.

US Electoral College: 270 of 538 needed to win. UK Parliament: 326 of 650 for majority. Most democracies use popular vote or proportional representation.
