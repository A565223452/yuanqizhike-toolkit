How to Use

  1. Click Choose a video file and select a video.
  2. Choose a Target resolution preset, or set custom width/height.
  3. Choose how to handle aspect ratio: Letterbox (preserve, add bars), Stretch (distort to fill), or Crop (fill, cut off edges).
  4. Use the video controls to seek to the desired frame.
  5. Click Capture Scaled Frame and download the resized image.

For full video resizing (not just frames), use a video editor like HandBrake or ffmpeg. This tool captures scaled still frames in real-time.
