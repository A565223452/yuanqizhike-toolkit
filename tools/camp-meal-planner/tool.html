How to Use

  1. Set number of people, days, and meals per day.
  2. Choose trip type (car/backpack/canoe).
  3. Click generate for meal plan.
  4. Use shopping list for packing.

Calories needed: 2500-3500 per person/day for camping. Bring 1 extra day of food. Use bear canister in bear country. Pack trash bags (leave no trace). Pre-measure spices. Use zip-lock bags for organization. Dry ice lasts longer than regular ice in coolers.
