How to Use

  1. Enter a non-negative integer (0 to 1000).
  2. See the exact factorial value (large numbers supported).
  3. View digit count, trailing zeros, and Stirling approximation.

n! = n × (n-1) × ... × 1. 0! = 1 by definition. Trailing zeros come from factors of 5 (paired with 2s). Stirling's approximation: n! ≈ √(2πn) × (n/e)^n.
