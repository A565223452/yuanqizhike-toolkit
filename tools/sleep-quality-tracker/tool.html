How to Use

  1. Set bedtime and wake time.
  2. Rate sleep quality (1-10).
  3. Count wakeups during the night.
  4. Rate morning mood (1-10).
  5. Add notes about caffeine, screen time, etc.
  6. Click "Save" to log entry.

Adults need 7-9 hours. Maintain consistent schedule (even weekends). Keep bedroom dark, cool (65-68°F/18-20°C), quiet. No screens 1 hour before bed (blue light). Avoid caffeine after 2pm. Exercise regularly but not within 3 hours of bedtime. Limit alcohol and nicotine.
