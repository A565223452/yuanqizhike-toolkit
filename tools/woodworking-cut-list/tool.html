How to Use

  1. Enter lumber dimensions: thickness, width, length (use actual, not nominal, sizes).
  2. Enter price per board foot from your lumber supplier.
  3. Set quantity of boards to purchase.
  4. Optionally, build a cut list: enter cut lengths and quantities, click + Add Cut.
  5. Click Calculate for board feet, cost, and optimized board layout.

Board feet (BF) = thickness(in) × width(in) × length(ft) ÷ 12. The cut list uses greedy bin-packing to minimize waste. Always buy 10-15% extra for mistakes and defects.
