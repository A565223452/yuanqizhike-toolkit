How to Use

  1. Type a task and select priority and optional due date.
  2. Press Enter or click Add.
  3. Click checkbox to mark done. Double-click text to edit.
  4. Use filter buttons to view All/Active/Done.

Tasks saved in browser localStorage — no signup, no server. Persists across sessions on same device/browser. Privacy: data never leaves your device. Clear browser data to reset. Priorities: High (red), Medium (orange), Low (green). Due dates highlight approaching deadlines.
