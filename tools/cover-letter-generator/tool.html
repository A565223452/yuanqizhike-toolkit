How to Use

  1. Enter your contact info (name, email, phone).
  2. Set date and hiring manager details.
  3. Fill in company, job title, years of experience.
  4. Write your key achievement with metrics.
  5. Explain why this company attracts you.
  6. Choose tone (formal, enthusiastic, concise).
  7. Click Generate then copy or download.

Personalize the letter with specific examples. Keep to 250-400 words (one page). Address hiring manager by name when possible - check LinkedIn for the name.
