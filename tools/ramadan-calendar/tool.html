How to Use

  1. Enter the year (2024-2035).
  2. Choose the calculation method for your region.
  3. Enter your latitude and longitude or click Use My Location for GPS.
  4. Click Get Ramadan Times.

Uses the AlAdhan API to fetch prayer times. Sample shows first 3 days. Click the link for the full month calendar. Always verify exact times with your local mosque.
