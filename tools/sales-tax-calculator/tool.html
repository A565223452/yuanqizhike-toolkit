How to Use

  1. Enter the Amount (before tax for forward, total for reverse).
  2. Pick a State to auto-fill the rate, or enter a custom rate.
  3. Choose mode: Add tax (forward) or Remove tax (reverse).
  4. See Base, Tax, and Total instantly.

State rates are the base statewide rate. Local cities and counties may add 1-4% on top. Use the Custom rate option to enter combined state + local rates.
