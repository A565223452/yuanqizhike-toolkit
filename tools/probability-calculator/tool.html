How to Use

  1. Single: enter favorable and total outcomes.
  2. Two Events: enter P(A), P(B), and select Independent/Dependent.
  3. For dependent events, also enter P(A|B).
  4. Click Calculate.

AND = both occur. OR = at least one occurs. Independent: P(A|B) = P(A). Dependent: needs P(A|B). Probabilities are 0-1 (0% to 100%).
