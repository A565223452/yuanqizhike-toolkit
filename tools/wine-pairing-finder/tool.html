How to Use

  1. Select your Food category (beef, fish, cheese, pasta, etc.).
  2. Choose the Cooking method (grilled, roasted, etc.).
  3. Optionally filter by Wine preference (red, white, sparkling, etc.).
  4. Set your Budget for tailored tips.
  5. Click Find Pairing for top wine recommendations with notes.

Pairings follow classic culinary principles: match intensity, contrast with acidity/sweetness, and consider regional traditions. Personal preference is always king.
