How to Use

  1. Choose test method (Cooper 12-min run, 1.5 mile, or Rockport walk).
  2. Enter your performance data.
  3. Select gender and age.
  4. See your VO2 max and fitness rating.

VO2 max = maximum oxygen uptake (ml/kg/min). Higher = better aerobic fitness. Elite athletes: 70-85 ml/kg/min. Sedentary: 25-35. Improves 5-25% with training. HIIT (4x4 min at 90-95% max HR) is most effective. Long slow distance also helps. Train 3-5x/week.
