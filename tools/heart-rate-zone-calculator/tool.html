How to Use

  1. Enter your age and resting heart rate (measure before getting out of bed).
  2. Choose a formula: 220-Age (classic), Tanaka (more accurate for older adults), Gulati (for women), or Hunt.
  3. Click Calculate Zones to see your 5 training zones.

Uses the Karvonen method (heart rate reserve) which factors in your resting HR for more personalized zones. Zone 2 (60-70% max HR) is ideal for fat burning and building aerobic base.
