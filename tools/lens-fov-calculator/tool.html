How to Use

  1. Enter Focal length in mm (e.g. 50, 24, 200).
  2. Select your Sensor size or use Custom for unusual formats.
  3. For custom sensors, enter Sensor width and height in mm.
  4. Set Focus distance in feet for field of view calculation.
  5. Optionally compare to another sensor format.

Angle of view is the angular extent visible through the lens. Field of view is the linear area captured at a specific distance. Crop factor converts focal length to its full-frame equivalent.
