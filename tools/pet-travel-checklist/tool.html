How to Use

  1. Select Pet type and Travel type (car, flight, camping).
  2. Choose Trip duration and Destination.
  3. Click Generate checklist for a comprehensive preparation list.
  4. Check off items as you complete them.

International travel needs 6+ months prep (rabies titer, import permits). Domestic flights need health certificate within 10 days. Brachycephalic breeds (pugs, bulldogs) cannot fly cargo.
