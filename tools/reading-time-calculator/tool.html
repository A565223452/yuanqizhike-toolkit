How to Use

  1. Paste or type text in the textarea.
  2. Choose reading speed (slow, average, fast).
  3. Choose speaking speed for presentation timing.
  4. Click Calculate for word count and time estimates.

Average adult reads 200-250 WPM silently. Speaking: 130 WPM (presentations), 150 WPM (audiobooks). Blog post: 500-800 words. TED talks: 18 min = ~2,300 words.
