How to Use

  1. Enter the vehicle price.
  2. Enter your down payment and/or trade-in value.
  3. Choose the loan term (24-84 months).
  4. Enter the interest rate (APR) from your lender.
  5. Set sales tax rate and any additional fees.
  6. Click Calculate Payment for monthly payment and full breakdown.

APR (Annual Percentage Rate) is the yearly interest rate. A lower credit score typically means a higher APR. Shop around for the best rate from banks, credit unions, and dealerships.
