How to Use

  1. Fill in your contact info at the top (name, title, email, phone, location).
  2. Write a brief Professional summary.
  3. Add Work Experience entries with role, company, period, and bullet points of achievements.
  4. Add Education entries with degree, school, and period.
  5. List your Skills comma-separated.
  6. Choose a Template (Modern/Classic/Minimal) and font size.
  7. Click Preview to see the result, or Print / Save as PDF to download.

All data stays in your browser. Three professional templates available. Print to PDF for a clean, ATS-friendly resume.
