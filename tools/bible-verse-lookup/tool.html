How to Use

  1. Select a book from the dropdown (Old or New Testament).
  2. Enter the chapter number.
  3. Optionally enter a verse number or range (e.g. 3 or 1-10).
  4. Click Look Up Verse to fetch the verse from bible-api.com (KJV).
  5. Or click Random Verse for an inspirational popular verse.

Requires internet connection to fetch verses. Uses the King James Version (KJV). Random verse uses a curated list of 30 popular verses that work offline.
