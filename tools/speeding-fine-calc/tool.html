How to Use

  1. Select state where ticket received.
  2. Enter speed limit and your speed.
  3. Choose zone type (school/work increases fines).
  4. Click Calculate Fine.

Fines vary by county within states. These are estimates. Insurance increases are the biggest long-term cost - 3 years of higher premiums. Traffic school can remove points in many states.
