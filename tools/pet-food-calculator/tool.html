How to Use

  1. Select pet type (dog or cat).
  2. Enter weight, age, activity level.
  3. Set food calorie density (kcal/cup).
  4. Choose body condition.
  5. Click Calculate.

RER = 70 × weight^0.75. DER = RER × activity multiplier. Dogs: 1.6-3.0×, Cats: 1.1-2.5×. Overweight pets: reduce 20-30%. Puppies eat more (growth). Treats <10% daily calories. Always transition food over 7 days. Consult vet for health conditions, pregnancy, or specialized diets.
