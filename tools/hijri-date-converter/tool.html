How to Use

  1. Enter Gregorian date or Hijri date.
  2. Click Note: Hijri or Note: Gregorian.
  3. View result with special occasions.

Hijri calendar is lunar: 12 months of 29-30 days = 354 days. Year shifts ~11 days earlier each Gregorian year. Important dates: Ramadan (month 9), Eid al-Fitr (10/1), Eid al-Adha (12/10), Islamic New Year (1/1), Ashura (1/10), Mawlid (3/12). Note: official dates depend on moon sighting.
