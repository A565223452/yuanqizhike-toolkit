How to Use

  1. Select your filing status.
  2. Enter your gross income (annual).
  3. Standard deduction auto-fills; override if you itemize.
  4. See your tax owed, marginal rate, and effective rate.

US federal taxes are progressive — only income in each bracket is taxed at that rate. So a single person making $75k pays 10% on the first $11,600, 12% on income from $11,600-$47,150, and 22% on income from $47,150-$75,000. Your "marginal rate" is your top bracket; your "effective rate" is your average. This is federal only — add state tax and 7.65% FICA.
