How to Use

  1. Enter tank length, width, and height.
  2. Select measurement unit (inches or cm).
  3. Enter substrate depth (gravel/sand at bottom).
  4. Set empty top space (water line below tank rim).
  5. Enter decorations displacement (rocks, wood displace water).
  6. Choose fish type for stocking recommendations.
  7. Click Calculate for volume, equipment, and stocking guide.

1 US gallon = 231 cubic inches = 3.785 liters. The "1 inch per gallon" rule is a starting point - research specific fish needs. Some fish (e.g. goldfish) produce more waste and need more space. Always cycle your tank before adding fish.
