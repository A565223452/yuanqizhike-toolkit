How to Use

  1. Enter your dog's birth date.
  2. Select dog size (affects schedule).
  3. Choose lifestyle (affects which vaccines are recommended).
  4. See full vaccination schedule with dates.

Core vaccines: DAPP (Distemper combo) and Rabies (required by law). Non-core: Bordetella (kennel cough), Leptospirosis, Lyme, Canine Influenza. Puppy series: 6-8 weeks, 10-12 weeks, 14-16 weeks. Adult boosters: annually or every 3 years depending on vaccine. Always consult your vet.
