How to Use

  1. Fill in personal info, summary, experience, education, skills.
  2. Format experience: Title | Company | Dates | Description.
  3. Choose a template (modern, classic, minimal).
  4. Click Download as PDF — print dialog opens.

Resume tips: Use action verbs (Led, Built, Improved). Quantify achievements ("Improved performance 40%"). Keep to 1 page if <10 years exp, 2 pages max. ATS-friendly: avoid images/tables, use standard fonts. Include keywords from job description. Save as PDF to preserve formatting.
