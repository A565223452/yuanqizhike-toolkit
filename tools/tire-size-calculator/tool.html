Tire Size Calculator

Compare tire sizes and dimensions. Calculate diameter, width, sidewall, and revolutions per mile. Free online tire size calculator.
Tire size format: WIDTH/ASPECT-RATIO[R][WHEEL-DIAMETER]
Example: 205/55R16 means 205mm wide, 55% aspect ratio, 16-inch wheel.
R = Radial construction.

How to Use

  1. Enter your original tire size (e.g. 205/55R16).
  2. Enter the new tire size you are considering.
  3. Click Compare Tires to see dimension differences and warnings.

Tire size format: WIDTH/ASPECT[R]WHEEL (e.g. 205/55R16). Diameter differences over 3% may affect ABS, traction control, and speedometer accuracy. Always verify fitment with a professional.
