How to Use

  1. Enter numbers separated by commas or spaces.
  2. Click Find Mode.
  3. View mode(s), frequency table, and other stats.

Mode = most frequent value. Dataset can be unimodal (1 mode), bimodal (2 modes), multimodal (many), or have no mode (all unique values).
