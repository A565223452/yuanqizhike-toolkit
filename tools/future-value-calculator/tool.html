How to Use

  1. Enter Initial investment (can be 0).
  2. Enter Annual contribution (regular deposits).
  3. Enter Annual rate (e.g., 7% for stock market average).
  4. Set Years and Compounding frequency.
  5. See the future value, total contributions, and interest earned.

Compound interest is the 8th wonder of the world. $10,000 invested at 7% for 30 years with $6,000/year contributions grows to over $700,000 - more than half is interest.
