How to Use

  1. Enter weight lifted and reps performed.
  2. Select unit (kg or lb).
  3. See estimated 1RM from 7 formulas.
  4. Use training percentages for programming.

7 formulas averaged: Epley, Brzycki, Lander, Lombardi, Mayhew, O'Conner, Wathen. Most accurate for 1-10 reps. Training zones: 90-100% strength (1-5 reps), 75-85% hypertrophy (6-12 reps), 50-70% endurance (15+ reps). Test actual 1RM with spotter.
