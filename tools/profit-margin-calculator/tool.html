How to Use

  1. Enter your Cost (what you pay for the product).
  2. Enter your Selling Price (what you charge customers).
  3. See live results: Profit, Margin %, Markup %, and ROI %.

Margin is profit as a percentage of selling price. Markup is profit as a percentage of cost. A 50% margin equals a 100% markup.
