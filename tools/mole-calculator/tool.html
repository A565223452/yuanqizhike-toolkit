How to Use

  1. Enter chemical formula (e.g., H2O, NaCl, C6H12O6).
  2. Change any value (mass, moles, molecules, or volume).
  3. All other values update automatically.
  4. See composition breakdown below.

1 mole = 6.022 × 10²³ particles (Avogadro's number). At STP (0°C, 1 atm), 1 mole of ideal gas = 22.4 L. Molar mass = sum of atomic masses. Use parentheses for groups: Ca(OH)2, Al2(SO4)3.
