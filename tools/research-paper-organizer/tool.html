How to Use

  1. Enter your Paper topic and choose Citation style (APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard).
  2. For each source, select Type (book, journal, website, news).
  3. Fill in Author, Title, Year, and publisher/URL.
  4. Add notes with key quotes, page numbers, or takeaways.
  5. Click + Add source to save. Generate bibliography when done.

Sources save in your browser session. Use Export to copy all data to clipboard for pasting into Word. Always verify generated citations against official style guides.
