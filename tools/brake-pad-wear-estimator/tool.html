How to Use

  1. Enter current and original pad thickness (mm).
  2. Enter current mileage and mileage at last replacement.
  3. Select driving style, vehicle type, and pad type.
  4. Enter annual mileage for time estimate.
  5. Click Estimate Wear for replacement timing.

Replace pads at 3mm thickness. Typical lifespan: 30-70k miles (city), 60-100k miles (highway). Ceramic pads last longer and produce less dust. EVs with regen braking can exceed 100k miles per pad set.
