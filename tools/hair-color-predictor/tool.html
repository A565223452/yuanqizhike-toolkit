How to Use

  1. Set your natural hair level (1-10).
  2. Choose desired level.
  3. Select hair condition.
  4. Click Predict Color.

Hair levels: 1=black, 10=lightest blonde. Developer: 10 vol = deposit/1 level, 20 vol = 1-2 levels, 30 vol = 2-3 levels, 40 vol = 3-4 levels (bleach). Don't lift >2 levels at home. Always strand test. Damaged hair needs gentler approach.
