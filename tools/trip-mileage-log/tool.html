How to Use

  1. Set the IRS mileage rate (67¢ for business in 2024).
  2. Enter trip details: date, type, odometer readings, purpose.
  3. Click + Add Trip to record each trip.
  4. Click Calculate Deduction for total tax deduction.

2024 IRS standard mileage rates: Business 67¢/mi, Medical/Moving 21¢/mi, Charity 14¢/mi. IRS requires a contemporaneous log with date, miles, destination, and business purpose. Personal miles are not deductible.
