How to Use

  1. Upload an image with a solid or simple background
  2. Pick the background color (click on image, use corner buttons, or auto-detect)
  3. Adjust tolerance and edge smoothing
  4. Click Remove Background
  5. Download the result as a transparent PNG

Works best with solid color backgrounds (green screen, white wall, etc.). For complex backgrounds, use a dedicated AI tool.
