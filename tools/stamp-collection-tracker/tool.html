How to Use

  1. Enter stamp details: country, year, Scott #, condition, value, and purchase price.
  2. Click Add to collection to save the entry.
  3. View summary of total stamps, catalog value, and gain/loss.
  4. Click Export CSV to download your collection.

Conditions: MNH (Mint Never Hinged) is most valuable. Catalog value is a guide, not sale price. Real value depends on market demand. Scott catalog is the US standard reference.
