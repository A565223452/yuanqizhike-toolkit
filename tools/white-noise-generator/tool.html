How to Use

  1. Select noise type: White, Pink, or Brown.
  2. Set volume (start at 30% to protect hearing).
  3. Choose duration (continuous or timed).
  4. Click Start to play.

White noise: equal energy all frequencies (TV static). Pink noise: equal per octave (rain-like, more natural). Brown noise: deeper (waterfall). Uses: sleep, focus, tinnitus relief, baby calming. Volume warning: keep under 60%, take breaks every 1-2 hr. Prolonged high volume can damage hearing.
