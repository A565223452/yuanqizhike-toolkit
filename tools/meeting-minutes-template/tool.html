How to Use

  1. Fill in meeting title, date, time, location.
  2. Enter attendees (one per line).
  3. List agenda items.
  4. For discussion, use format: Discussion point; Decision made.
  5. For action items, use format: Task | Owner | Due date.
  6. Click Generate to preview, Print to save as PDF.

Tip: meeting minutes should be circulated within 24 hours. Always assign clear owners and deadlines for action items. For recurring meetings, save a template to reuse the structure.
