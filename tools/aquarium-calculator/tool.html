How to Use

  1. Select tank shape and enter dimensions.
  2. Set substrate depth and water change %.
  3. Choose fish type for stocking.
  4. Click Calculate.

Volume = L × W × H ÷ 231 (gallons). Tropical: 1 inch fish per gallon. Goldfish: 1 inch per 2 gallons (heavy waste). Marine: 1 inch per 4 gallons. Filter: 4× tank volume per hour. Heater: 5W per gallon. Cycle tank 4-6 weeks before fish. Weekly 25% water changes. Stock at 60-70% of max for safety.
