How to Use

  1. Enter horse weight in lbs or kg.
  2. Select activity level and life stage.
  3. Choose hay type (grass, legume, or mixed).
  4. Choose if grain/concentrate is fed.
  5. Click Calculate Feed for daily requirements.

Based on NRC Nutrient Requirements of Horses. Total feed: 1.5-3% body weight, with forage minimum 1% BW. Grain limited to 4 lbs per meal to prevent colic/founder. Adjust by individual metabolism and body condition score (5 = ideal).
