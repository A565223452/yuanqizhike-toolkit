How to Use

  1. Enter your Page URL (will display as breadcrumb).
  2. Choose Desktop or Mobile preview.
  3. Write your Title tag (50-60 chars, 600px max).
  4. Write your Meta description (150-160 chars).
  5. See the live Google SERP preview and check for truncation.

Google uses pixel width, not character count, to truncate titles and descriptions. Special characters like W, M take more pixels than i, l. Test both desktop and mobile to ensure your snippet looks good everywhere.
