How to Use

  1. Pick a UUID version: v4 (random), v1 (timestamp), or Nil.
  2. Set Quantity (up to 1000).
  3. Toggle Uppercase, braces {}, or no hyphens.
  4. Click Generate, then Copy All or Download.

UUIDs are 128-bit identifiers, unique across space and time. v4 uses cryptographic random. v1 includes a timestamp. Collision probability is astronomically low. RFC 4122 compliant.
