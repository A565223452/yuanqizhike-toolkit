How to Use

  1. Enter air temperature.
  2. Enter relative humidity (%).
  3. Choose temperature unit.
  4. See dew point and comfort level.

Dew point is the temperature at which air becomes saturated (100% humidity). Magnus formula: Td = (b·α)/(a-α) where α = ln(RH/100) + (a·T)/(b+T). Lower dew point = drier air. Comfortable range: 10-18°C. Above 24°C is oppressive. Wet bulb temperature is the lowest temperature achievable by evaporative cooling.
