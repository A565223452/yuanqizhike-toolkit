How to Use

  1. Specify what lens is designed for (FF, APS-C, M4/3).
  2. Enter focal length marked on lens.
  3. Select camera body you are using.
  4. Click Calculate for effective focal length.

Crop factors: Full Frame = 1x, APS-C Canon = 1.6x, APS-C Nikon/Sony = 1.5x, Micro 4/3 = 2x. A 50mm on APS-C acts like 75mm on full frame.
