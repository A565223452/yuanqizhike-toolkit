How to Use

  1. Name your deck (e.g. Spanish verbs, Anatomy terms).
  2. Type Front (question) and Back (answer).
  3. Click Add card.
  4. Use Prev / Flip / Next to study. Click card to flip.
  5. Click Shuffle to randomize order.
  6. Click Save deck to store locally; Load deck to retrieve.
  7. Click Export JSON to download your deck.

Perfect for memorization: vocabulary, definitions, history dates, medical terms, and more.
