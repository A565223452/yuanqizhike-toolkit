How to Use

  1. Search by country name.
  2. Filter to see only 110V or 220V countries.
  3. Reference voltage and frequency (Hz) standards.

Most laptops/phones are dual voltage (100-240V) -only need plug adapter. Hair dryers, irons, kettles are often single-voltage — need a converter (or buy local). Frequency (50Hz vs 60Hz) matters for motors/clocks. Always check device label: "INPUT: 100-240V" means universal; "INPUT: 120V" means US only.
