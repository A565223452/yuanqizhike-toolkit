How to Use

  1. Enter distance and elevation gain.
  2. Select temperature, intensity, sun exposure.
  3. Enter your body weight.
  4. Click Calculate Water for amount needed.

General rule: 0.5L per hour moderate hiking. Adjust for heat (+50%), cold (-30%), and exertion. Always carry more than estimated for safety. Filter natural water.
