How to Use

  1. Enter the Air temperature (F or C).
  2. Enter the Relative humidity percentage.
  3. Enter the Wind speed and unit.
  4. Set Solar radiation (use preset for shade/cloudy/sunny).
  5. Click Calculate Feels-Like for the apparent temperature.

Three methods: NWS Wind Chill (cold + wind below 50F), NWS Heat Index (heat + humidity above 80F), and Australian Apparent Temperature (comprehensive, all conditions). The calculator auto-selects the appropriate method.
