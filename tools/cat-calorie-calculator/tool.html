How to Use

  1. Enter your cat's weight in pounds.
  2. Select age category.
  3. Choose activity level.
  4. Select body condition (ideal, overweight, etc.).
  5. See daily calorie needs and food portions.

Cat calorie formula: RER = 70 × (weight in kg)^0.75. Multipliers: kitten 2.5-3.0×, neutered adult 1.2×, intact adult 1.4×, overweight 0.8×, pregnant 2.0×. Average adult cat: 200-300 kcal/day. Dry food ~300 kcal/cup, wet food ~80 kcal/3oz can. Always consult your vet.
