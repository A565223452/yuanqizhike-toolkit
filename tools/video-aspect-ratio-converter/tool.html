How to Use

  1. Click Choose a video file and select a video.
  2. Choose Target aspect ratio: 16:9 (YouTube), 9:16 (TikTok/Reels), 1:1 (Instagram), 4:5 (Facebook), etc.
  3. Choose Fit mode: Contain (letterbox), Cover (crop), or Blur (blurred background + contain).
  4. Set background color and output dimensions.
  5. Use video controls to seek to desired frame.
  6. Click Capture Converted Frame and download.

For permanent video aspect ratio conversion (creating a new video), use a video editor. This tool captures converted still frames in real-time, perfect for thumbnails and preview images for social media platforms.
