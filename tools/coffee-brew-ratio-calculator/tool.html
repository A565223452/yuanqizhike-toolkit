How to Use

  1. Select brew method (pour over, French press, espresso, etc.).
  2. Choose what to input: cups, grams of coffee, or grams of water.
  3. Enter the amount.
  4. Click Calculate for complete recipe.

Golden ratios: Pour over 1:16, French press 1:15, Espresso 1:2, AeroPress 1:14, Cold brew 1:8 (concentrate). Adjust to taste - stronger = more coffee or less water.
