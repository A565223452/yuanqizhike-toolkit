How to Use

  1. Add your questions and answers.
  2. Click + Add question for more entries.
  3. Click Copy JSON-LD to get the structured data.
  4. Paste it inside your page's <head>.
  5. Test with Google's Rich Results Test.

FAQ schema can get your site featured in Google rich results, showing expandable Q&A in search results. Eligibility: Q&A must be visible on page, factual, not promotional. After deploying, monitor Search Console for "FAQ rich results" report.
