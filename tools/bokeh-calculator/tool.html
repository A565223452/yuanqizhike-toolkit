How to Use

  1. Enter Focal length (e.g. 50, 85, 135mm) and Aperture (f/1.4 to f/16).
  2. Set Subject distance and Background distance in feet.
  3. Choose Sensor type to auto-fill circle of confusion.
  4. Click Calculate for DOF and background blur analysis.

Blur circle larger than circle of confusion (CoC) creates visible bokeh. Larger blur relative to CoC means creamier background. Full-frame sensors produce more background blur than crop sensors at equivalent framing.
