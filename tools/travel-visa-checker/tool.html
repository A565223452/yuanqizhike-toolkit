How to Use

  1. Select your passport country.
  2. Choose your destination.
  3. Click Check visa requirement for a single destination.
  4. Click Show all destinations for a full comparison table.

Visa statuses: visa-free, ETA/e-visa, visa on arrival, or visa required. Always confirm with official embassy sources before travel - rules change frequently.
