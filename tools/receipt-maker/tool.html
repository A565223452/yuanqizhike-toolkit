How to Use

  1. Fill in Receipt # and Date.
  2. Enter your business info and the payor's name.
  3. Choose Payment method (Cash, Card, etc.).
  4. Add line items with description and amount.
  5. Set tax % and payment status (Paid/Partial/Unpaid).
  6. Click Print / Save as PDF to download.

All data is processed locally. Perfect for freelancers, small businesses, and service providers needing quick printable receipts.
