How to Use

  1. Enter cron expression (5 fields).
  2. Or pick from examples.
  3. Click Convert.

Cron format: minute hour day-of-month month day-of-week. * = any, */N = every N, 1-5 = range, 1,3,5 = list. Sunday = 0 or 7. Common: "0 9 * * 1-5" = weekdays 9AM. "*/15 * * * *" = every 15 min. "0 0 1 * *" = monthly midnight.
