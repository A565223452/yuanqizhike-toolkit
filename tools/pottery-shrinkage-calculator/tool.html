How to Use

  1. Select clay body (earthenware, stoneware, porcelain, raku) or custom.
  2. Confirm shrinkage rate (auto-filled, adjust if needed).
  3. Enter target fired size and choose unit.
  4. Choose calculation mode: target-to-wet, wet-to-fired, or reverse.
  5. Click Calculate for wet size needed.

Shrinkage formula: wet = fired / (1 - rate/100). Always test your clay batch - shrinkage varies by 1-2% even within the same clay body. Bisque firing causes most shrinkage; glaze firing adds the rest.
