How to Use

  1. Enter first day of last period.
  2. Set cycle length (typically 21-35 days).
  3. See ovulation date and fertile window.

Ovulation occurs ~14 days before next period. Fertile window: 5 days before + ovulation day + 1 day after (sperm survives 5 days, egg 12-24 hr). Best conception day: day before ovulation. For regular 28-day cycle: ovulation on day 14. Cycles vary — track for 3+ months for accuracy.
