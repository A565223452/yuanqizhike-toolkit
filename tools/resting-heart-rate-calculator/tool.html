How to Use

  1. Enter your resting heart rate (or use the measure tool).
  2. Set age, gender, and activity level.
  3. See your fitness rating and exercise zones.
  4. For measurement: sit quietly 5 min, find pulse, tap button each beat for 30s.

RHR norms: Adults 60-100 bpm. Athletes 40-60 bpm. Lower RHR generally indicates better cardiovascular fitness. Measure in morning before rising. Avoid caffeine/nicotine. Stress and medications affect readings. Consult doctor if consistently above 100 or below 40 bpm.
