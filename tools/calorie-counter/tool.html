How to Use

  1. Set your daily calorie goal (default 2000).
  2. Choose the date to log (defaults to today).
  3. Type a food name (autocomplete suggests common foods with calories/macros) or enter your own.
  4. Enter calories, protein, carbs, fat.
  5. Select the meal (breakfast/lunch/dinner/snack) and servings.
  6. Click + Add to Log.
  7. Use Clear Day to reset the day's entries.

Data is saved locally in your browser (localStorage). Each day is logged separately. Selecting a suggested food auto-fills its nutrition values.
