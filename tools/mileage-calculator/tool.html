How to Use

  1. Set the reimbursement rate ($/mile) -IRS standard business rate is $0.67/mile for 2024.
  2. Log each trip with date, purpose, locations, and miles.
  3. Click Calculate totals for reimbursement amount.
  4. Click Export as CSV to download the log.

IRS mileage rates 2024: Business $0.67/mile, Medical/Moving $0.21/mile, Charity $0.14/mile. Keep detailed logs for tax deduction.
