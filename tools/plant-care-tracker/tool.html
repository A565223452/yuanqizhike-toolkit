How to Use

  1. Click "+ Add Plant" to add a new plant.
  2. Enter name, species, watering frequency.
  3. Set last watered date.
  4. Click "Water Now" when you water a plant.
  5. Status shows: Good (green), Soon (yellow), Overdue (red).

Plant care tips: Check soil moisture before watering (overwatering kills more plants than underwatering). Most houseplants need water when top inch of soil is dry. Bright indirect light is ideal for most. Fertilize monthly in growing season. Repot every 1-2 years.
