How to Use

  1. Select gender, age.
  2. Enter height and weight (switch units if needed).
  3. Choose activity level.
  4. Click Calculate.

BMR = calories burned at rest. TDEE = BMR × activity factor. To lose weight: eat 500 cal below TDEE. To gain: 250-500 cal above. Macro split: protein 1.6-2.2g/kg, fat 25% of calories, rest carbs. Mifflin-St Jeor is most accurate for general use.
