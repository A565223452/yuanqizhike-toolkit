How to Use

  1. Select the activity from the dropdown (100+ options across categories).
  2. Enter your body weight in kg or lb.
  3. Enter the duration in minutes or hours.
  4. View the estimated calories burned.

Formula: Calories = MET × weight(kg) × hours. MET values sourced from the Compendium of Physical Activities. Estimates are approximate — actual burn varies with fitness level, body composition, terrain, and intensity.
