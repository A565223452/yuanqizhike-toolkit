How to Use

  1. Pick a vehicle preset or enter battery size and efficiency manually.
  2. Set current charge (%) to estimate remaining range.
  3. Choose driving environment (city, mixed, highway) and style (eco, normal, sport).
  4. Adjust temperature and climate control — cold weather and heating can reduce range by 20–0%.
  5. Click Calculate Range for a detailed breakdown.

Typical EV efficiency: 3- mi/kWh. Cold weather (below 32°F) can cut range 30%+. Highway speeds above 65 mph reduce efficiency significantly. Preconditioning the battery while plugged in helps in cold weather.
