How to Use

  1. Enter the Wind speed value.
  2. Select the Unit (mph, km/h, m/s, or knots).
  3. Click Convert to Beaufort for the Beaufort force, name, and effects.

The Beaufort scale (0-12) was developed in 1805 by Sir Francis Beaufort. It relates wind speed to observed conditions on land and sea. Force 12 = hurricane force (64+ knots / 73+ mph).
