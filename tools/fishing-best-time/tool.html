How to Use

  1. Set the Date for your fishing trip.
  2. Enter your Timezone offset, Latitude, and Longitude (or use NYC defaults).
  3. Click Find best times.
  4. Review major and minor feeding periods, sunrise/sunset, and tomorrow forecast.

Based on solunar theory (John Alden Knight). New and Full Moon days get the highest bite ratings. Always check local weather and regulations.
