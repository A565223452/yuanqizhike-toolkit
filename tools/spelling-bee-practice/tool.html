How to Use

  1. Select Difficulty from grade 1 to spelling bee championship level.
  2. Choose how many words to practice.
  3. Click Start practice - words are spoken aloud automatically.
  4. Use buttons to hear word again, see definition, or hear in sentence.
  5. Type the spelling and submit. Skip if unsure.
  6. Review your results at the end.

Uses browser speech synthesis. Enable your speakers. Spelling bee level includes national winning words like cymotrichous and appoggiatura.
