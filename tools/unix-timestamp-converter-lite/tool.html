How to Use

  1. Enter a Unix timestamp or click Now.
  2. Click to convert to human-readable date.
  3. Or enter a date and click to get timestamp.
  4. Choose format and timezone offset.

Unix timestamp = seconds since Jan 1, 1970 00:00:00 UTC (the "epoch"). Used in every programming language. Will overflow in 2038 on 32-bit systems (Y2K38 problem). 64-bit systems are safe until year 292 billion.
