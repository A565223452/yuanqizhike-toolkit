How to Use

  1. Set Deck size (60 standard, 40 limited, 100 commander).
  2. Enter Average CMC (converted mana cost) of your spells.
  3. Choose Number of colors and count of ramp/dorks.
  4. Adjust the mana curve inputs for each CMC slot.
  5. Click Analyze deck for land count and color distribution.

Aggro decks want 20-22 lands, midrange 23-25, control 26-28. Add fetches and shocks for multi-color consistency. Aim for 16+ cards at CMC 1-2 in aggro.
