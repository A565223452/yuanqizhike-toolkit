How to Use

  1. Enter trip distance (miles or km).
  2. Set car MPG or L/100km.
  3. Enter gas price.
  4. Choose one-way or round trip.

Average US MPG: 25 (cars), 18 (SUVs), 22 (trucks). Gasoline produces 8.89 kg CO2 per gallon. EVs are 3-4x more efficient: ~30 kWh/100mi vs ~33 kWh/100mi for gas (after power plant losses). Plan rest stops every 2 hours on long trips.
