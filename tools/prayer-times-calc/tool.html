How to Use

  1. Enter latitude/longitude of your location.
  2. Select calculation method for your region.
  3. Choose Asr method (Standard or Hanafi).
  4. Set date and timezone offset.
  5. Click Calculate Prayer Times.

Find coordinates with Google Maps (right-click). Prayer times are approximations. For accurate times, consult local mosque or islamicfinder.com.
