How to Use

  1. Choose category (top, bottom, dress, shoes, etc.).
  2. Enter color and name for each item.
  3. Click Add Item to add to your closet.
  4. Click Generate Outfit for random outfit suggestion.

Items saved in your browser (localStorage). Use clear button to reset. Build capsule wardrobe: 30 items = mix-and-match possibilities.
