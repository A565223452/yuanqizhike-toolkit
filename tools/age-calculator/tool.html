How to Use

  1. Enter your birth date.
  2. Optionally change the Age at date (defaults to today).
  3. See your exact age in years, months, and days.
  4. Get fun stats: total days, heartbeats, breaths, and next birthday countdown.

Age calculations account for leap years and varying month lengths. Heartbeats assume 70 bpm average. The "half birthday" is 6 months after your actual birthday.
