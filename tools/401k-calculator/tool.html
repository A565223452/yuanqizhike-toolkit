How to Use

  1. Enter your Current 401k balance.
  2. Enter your Annual salary.
  3. Set your contribution % (10-15% recommended).
  4. Set Employer match % (typical: 3-6%).
  5. Enter expected Annual return (7% is S&P 500 average).
  6. Set Years to retirement.

Always contribute at least enough to get the full employer match - it is free money. The 4% rule suggests you can withdraw 4% of your balance annually in retirement without running out for 30 years.
