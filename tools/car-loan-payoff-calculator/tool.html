How to Use

  1. Enter loan balance, interest rate, and monthly payment.
  2. Add extra monthly payment if any.
  3. Optionally add one-time lump sum payment.
  4. Choose biweekly payments if applicable.
  5. Click Calculate Payoff for accelerated schedule.

Biweekly payments (half payment every 2 weeks) result in 26 payments/year = 13 monthly payments, saving 4-6 years on a 30-year loan. Round up to nearest $50 for small automatic savings.
