How to Use

  1. Enter your latitude (deg N) and longitude (deg E), or select a city.
  2. Choose true north or magnetic north.
  3. If using magnetic compass, enter magnetic declination for your area.
  4. Click Find Qibla Direction for bearing and visual compass.

Qibla is the direction Muslims face during prayer, pointing toward the Kaaba in Mecca, Saudi Arabia (21.4225N, 39.8262E). Magnetic declination varies by location - check NOAA for your area.
