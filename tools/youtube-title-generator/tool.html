How to Use

  1. Enter your Video topic (e.g., "how to bake sourdough").
  2. Choose a Style: how-to, listicle, question, curiosity, etc.
  3. Click Generate Titles for 5 title ideas.
  4. Click Copy on any title, or Copy All for the full list.

Ideal YouTube title length is 60-70 characters. The first 50 chars carry the most SEO weight. Numbers and brackets boost CTR by 10-30%. Avoid ALL CAPS and excessive punctuation.
