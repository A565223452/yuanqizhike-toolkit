How to Use

  1. Enter trip distance in miles or km.
  2. Set your car's fuel efficiency (MPG, L/100km, or km/L).
  3. Enter the gas price per gallon or liter.
  4. Set passengers to split costs.

Average US MPG: 25 (cars), 18 (SUVs), 22 (trucks). Highway MPG is typically 20-30% higher than city. EVs use ~30 kWh/100mi — multiply by your electricity rate (~$0.15/kWh) for cost.
