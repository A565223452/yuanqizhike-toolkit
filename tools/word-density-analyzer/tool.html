How to Use

  1. Paste your content into the textarea.
  2. Set minimum word length, n-gram size (1, 2, or 3), and how many top results to show.
  3. Toggle remove stop words and case sensitivity.
  4. Click Analyze to view word frequency, density, and SEO recommendations.

For SEO, aim for keyword density of 1-3% for primary keywords. Densities above 5% may be flagged as keyword stuffing. Use bigrams and trigrams to find common phrases.
