How to Use

  1. Choose mode: Wake up at (find bedtime) or Go to bed now (find wake time).
  2. Enter target time.
  3. See recommended times based on 90-min sleep cycles.

Sleep cycles last ~90 minutes (5 stages). Waking at cycle end (after 5-6 cycles) reduces grogginess. Adults need 7-9 hours. Caffeine half-life: 5-6 hours. Blue light from screens suppresses melatonin. Most restorative sleep: 10pm-midnight.
