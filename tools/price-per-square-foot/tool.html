How to Use

  1. Enter price and size for property 1.
  2. Optionally enter property 2 to compare.
  3. Select currency and unit (sq ft or sq m).
  4. See price per sq ft and per sq m.

Price per sq ft is the standard way to compare property values. It accounts for size differences. Keep in mind: lot size, age, condition, location, and amenities all affect value. Always research recent sales (comps) in the neighborhood for accurate comparison.
