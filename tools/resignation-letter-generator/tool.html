How to Use

  1. Fill in your name, address and contact details.
  2. Enter manager name and company details.
  3. Set your job title and dates.
  4. Choose notice period (check your contract).
  5. Optionally select a reason for leaving.
  6. Add personal note if desired.
  7. Click Generate then copy or download.

Tell your manager in person first. Check your contract for required notice. Keep tone professional - this letter goes in your HR file. Don't burn bridges!
