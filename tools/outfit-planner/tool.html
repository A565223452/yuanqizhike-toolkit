How to Use

  1. Enter temperature and weather condition.
  2. Select occasion, gender, and style.
  3. Click Plan Outfit.

Outfits categorized by temperature: freezing (<20°F), cold (20-40°F), cool (40-55°F), mild (55-70°F), warm (70-85°F), hot (>85°F). Layer for flexibility. Natural fabrics breathe better. Dark colors for cold, light for hot. Rainy: water-resistant gear. Sunny: UV protection. Accessorize to express personality.
