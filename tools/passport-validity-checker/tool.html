How to Use

  1. Enter passport issue date and expiration date.
  2. Set travel date and return date.
  3. Select destination region (different rules apply).
  4. Choose your passport issuing country.
  5. Click Check Passport for validity analysis.

Most countries require 6 months validity beyond entry. Schengen requires 3 months beyond exit. Some countries (Canada, UK, Australia) only require duration of stay. Always verify with the destination embassy before travel.
