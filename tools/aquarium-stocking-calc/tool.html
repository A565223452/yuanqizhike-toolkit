How to Use

  1. Enter Tank size in gallons.
  2. Select Filtration, plant density, and water change schedule.
  3. Click + Add fish to add each species in your tank.
  4. Choose species and enter count.
  5. Click Calculate stocking for bio-load analysis.

1 inch/gallon is the conservative rule. Goldfish produce 8x more waste than tropical fish. Schooling species need 6+ to thrive. Distribute fish across top/mid/bottom levels.
