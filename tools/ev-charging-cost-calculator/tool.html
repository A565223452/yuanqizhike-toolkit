How to Use

  1. Enter battery size in kWh and current/target charge %.
  2. Confirm charging efficiency (default 90%).
  3. Set electricity rate or choose charging location (auto-filled).
  4. Enter vehicle efficiency (mi/kWh) and miles/month.
  5. Click Calculate for session and monthly costs.

EV rates: Home ~$0.15/kWh, Public L2 ~$0.25/kWh, DC Fast ~$0.40/kWh. Average EV gets 3-4 mi/kWh. Charging losses are 10-15%. Off-peak home charging saves 50% vs peak.
