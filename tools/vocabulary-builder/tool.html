How to Use

  1. Type a new word and optional definition.
  2. Click Add word.
  3. Click Review due to study words scheduled for today.
  4. Click Show definition to check yourself.
  5. Grade your recall: Again (1d), Hard (3d), Good (7d), or Easy (14d). The schedule updates automatically.
  6. Click Export to download your words as CSV.

Uses spaced repetition (like Anki). Words you struggle with appear sooner; easy words come back later. Data saved locally in your browser only.
