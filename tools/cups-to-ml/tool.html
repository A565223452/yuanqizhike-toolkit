How to Use

  1. Type cups or milliliters — they sync automatically.
  2. Select cup standard: US (240ml), US customary (236.6ml), Metric (250ml), UK (284ml), etc.
  3. See all volume conversions.

The US has two cup sizes: "legal" (240ml, used in nutrition labels) and "customary" (236.6ml, used in recipes). Most US recipes use customary. Metric cup (250ml) is used in Australia, New Zealand, Canada, and South Africa. UK cup is now metric; imperial cups are rarely used.
