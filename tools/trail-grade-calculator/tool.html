How to Use

  1. Enter horizontal distance and elevation gain.
  2. See trail grade, slope angle, and difficulty.
  3. Use hiking time estimator below for trip planning.

Trail grade = (elevation / distance) × 100%. Difficulty: <5% easy, 5-10% moderate, 10-15% strenuous, 15-25% very steep, >40% technical. Naismith's rule: 1 hour per 5 km + 1 hour per 600m elevation gain. Add 30 min for breaks every 2 hours.
