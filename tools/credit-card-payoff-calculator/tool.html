How to Use

  1. Enter your credit card balance.
  2. Enter the APR (annual interest rate).
  3. Set the minimum payment % (usually 2-3%).
  4. Enter the monthly payment you can afford.
  5. Compare minimum-only vs your payment.

Credit card interest compounds daily. Minimum payments are designed to keep you in debt for decades. Paying just $50-100 extra per month can save thousands and years. Always pay more than the minimum — even small extra payments make a big difference.
