How to Use

  1. Choose plan duration (30-730 days).
  2. Select reading style.
  3. Set days per week.
  4. Choose start date.
  5. Click Generate Plan or Show Next 7 Days.

Bible has 1,189 chapters total. At 3-4 chapters/day, you finish in 1 year. Use Bible apps (YouVersion, Bible Gateway) for daily reminders.
