How to Use

  1. Click Choose a video file and select a video.
  2. Choose Playback speed from 0.25x (very slow) to 4x (very fast), or use a custom value.
  3. Toggle Pitch correction to preserve original pitch (vs. chipmunk effect at fast speeds).
  4. Play the video and use the slider to seek.
  5. Click Capture Frame at Current Position to save a still image.

For permanent speed change of a video file (creating a new video), use a video editor. This tool previews the video at custom speeds and captures frames in real-time.
