How to Use

  1. Select dog size (affects aging rate).
  2. Enter age in years or birth date.
  3. Click Calculate.

Modern formula (AVMA 2019): Year 1 = 15, Year 2 = +9 (24 total), then +4-7 per year depending on size. Smaller dogs live longer (15 yr) than giant breeds (9 yr). The old "7×" rule is inaccurate — dogs mature faster initially. Senior stage starts at ~6-9 years depending on size. Geriatric care: twice-yearly vet visits, joint supplements, softer food.
