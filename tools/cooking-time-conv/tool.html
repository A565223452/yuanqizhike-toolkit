How to Use

  1. Enter the original recipe time and select minutes or hours.
  2. Choose the Original method used in the recipe.
  3. Choose the Convert TO method you want to use.
  4. Select the food type for more accurate conversion.
  5. Click Convert Time to get the adjusted cooking time.

Conversions are approximate. Air fryers cook ~25% faster than ovens. Slow cookers use LOW setting by default. Always verify doneness with a meat thermometer.
