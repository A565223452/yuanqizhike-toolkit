How to Use

  1. Select your face shape and hair type.
  2. Enter current length (measure from crown to tips).
  3. Choose target length from pixie to hip-length.
  4. Set growth rate and trim frequency.
  5. Click Calculate for time estimate and styling tips.

Hair grows ~1.25 cm (0.5 in) per month. Curly and coily hair appear 30-50% shorter when dry (shrinkage). For accurate measurement, measure when hair is wet and straight.
