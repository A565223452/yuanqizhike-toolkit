How to Use

  1. Enter latitude/longitude and UTC offset for your location.
  2. Select date for prayer times.
  3. Choose calculation method (MWL, ISNA, Egyptian, etc.).
  4. Choose Asr method (Standard or Hanafi).
  5. Click Calculate Prayer Times.

Prayer times are calculated using astronomical algorithms. Methods differ by region: ISNA (North America), MWL (Europe, parts of Asia), Egyptian (Africa, Syria, Lebanon), Umm al-Qura (Saudi Arabia), Karachi (Pakistan, India, Bangladesh).
