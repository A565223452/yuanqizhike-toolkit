How to Use

  1. Select home and destination timezones.
  2. Enter departure time and flight duration.
  3. Choose direction (or auto-detect).
  4. Click Calculate.

Jet lag recovery: ~1 day per timezone (eastward), 1.5 days (westward). East travel is harder (advancing sleep = harder than delaying). Light exposure is the strongest time cue: morning light advances clock, evening light delays. Melatonin 0.5-3mg helps reset. Hydrate, avoid alcohol, adopt local schedule immediately.
