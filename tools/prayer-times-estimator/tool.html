How to Use

  1. Select calculation method (different Islamic schools).
  2. Enter your latitude and longitude.
  3. Set timezone and day of year.
  4. Click Calculate for prayer schedule.

Five daily prayers: Fajr (dawn), Dhuhr (noon), Asr (afternoon), Maghrib (sunset), Isha (night). Times vary by location and season. Always confirm with local Islamic authority.
