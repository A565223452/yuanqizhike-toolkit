How to Use

  1. Enter Roof length and Roof width (building footprint).
  2. Select Roof pitch (rise over 12 inches).
  3. Choose Number of sides (gable = 2, hip = 4).
  4. Set Waste factor (10% recommended) and Bundle cost.
  5. Click Calculate.

Pitch multiplier accounts for slope length. 6/12 pitch adds ~12% surface area. Buy starter strip, ridge caps, underlayment, drip edge, and nails separately.
