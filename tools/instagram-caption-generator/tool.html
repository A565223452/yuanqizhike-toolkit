How to Use

  1. Enter your Topic (e.g., "beach sunset", "coffee morning", "new puppy").
  2. Pick a Mood: happy, inspirational, funny, romantic, etc.
  3. Click Generate Caption for a ready-to-post caption.
  4. Click again for a different variation.
  5. Click Copy Caption when you find one you like.

Instagram captions allow up to 2,200 characters. The first 125 chars show before "more". Use 5-10 hashtags for best engagement. Posts with emojis get 15% higher engagement.
