How to Use

  1. Knit a gauge swatch and count stitches per 4 inches and rows per 4 inches.
  2. Enter your target width and length for the project.
  3. Choose the unit (cm or inches).
  4. Optionally select yarn weight for a yardage estimate.
  5. Click Calculate to see cast-on stitches, total rows, and yarn needed.

Always knit a gauge swatch first! Gauge varies between knitters, needle materials, and yarn brands. Buy 10-15% extra yarn as insurance.
