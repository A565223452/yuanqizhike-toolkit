How to Use

  1. Choose Pay period (weekly, bi-weekly, etc.) and Pay date.
  2. Enter Employer info (company name, EIN, address).
  3. Enter Employee info (name, ID, position, filing status).
  4. Enter Earnings: hourly rate, hours, overtime, bonus.
  5. Set Deductions: federal/state tax %, SS, Medicare, insurance, 401(k).
  6. Click Preview or Print / Save as PDF.

Tax percentages are simplified estimates. For exact withholding, consult IRS Publication 15 (Circular E) and your state's tax tables.
