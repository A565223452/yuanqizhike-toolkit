How to Use

  1. Enter trip distance and your vehicle's MPG.
  2. Set current gas price per gallon.
  3. Add tolls, parking, and wear cost per mile.
  4. Click Calculate for total trip cost.

Typical wear/maintenance cost: $0.05-0.15/mi. IRS reimbursement rate ~$0.67/mi (covers all costs). For EVs, estimate $0.04/mi for electricity instead of gas.
