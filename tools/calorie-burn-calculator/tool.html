How to Use

  1. Choose activity type.
  2. Enter duration and your weight.
  3. Select intensity level.
  4. See calories burned and food equivalents.

Formula: Calories = MET × weight(kg) × time(hours). MET = Metabolic Equivalent of Task. 1 MET = resting metabolic rate. Higher intensity and body weight = more calories burned. 1 lb fat = 3500 calories. Mix cardio and strength training for best results. Diet has bigger impact than exercise alone.
