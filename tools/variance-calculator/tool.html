How to Use

  1. Enter numbers separated by commas or spaces.
  2. Click Calculate Variance.
  3. View both sample (n-1) and population (N) variance.

Variance measures how far data points spread from the mean. Lower = data clusters near mean. Higher = more spread out. Standard deviation is √variance.
