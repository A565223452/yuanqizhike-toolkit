How to Use

  1. Select mystery type (auto-detected for today, or choose manually).
  2. Click Start Rosary to begin.
  3. Read each prayer, then click Next Prayer.
  4. Complete all 5 mysteries (53 Hail Marys, 6 Our Fathers, 6 Glory Bes).

Rosary schedule: Joyful (Mon/Sat), Sorrowful (Tue/Fri), Glorious (Wed/Sun), Luminous (Thu). Each decade = 1 Our Father + 10 Hail Marys + 1 Glory Be, meditating on one mystery.
