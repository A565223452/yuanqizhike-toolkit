How to Use

  1. Select your Filing Status (Single, Married, or Head of Household).
  2. Enter your Annual Income.
  3. The Standard Deduction auto-fills based on status; adjust if using itemized deductions.
  4. Click Calculate Tax to see tax owed, effective rate, and bracket breakdown.

Uses 2024 US federal tax brackets. Does not include state taxes, FICA, or credits. Estimate only.
