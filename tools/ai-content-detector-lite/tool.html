How to Use

  1. Paste text into the box (minimum 50 words recommended).
  2. Click Analyze to evaluate AI probability.
  3. Or click Load sample to test with sample AI-style text.
  4. Review the detailed breakdown of detected signals.

Heuristic-based detection using 7 signals: sentence length variance, vocabulary diversity, long word ratio, punctuation density, sentence start repetition, bigram repetition, and formal connective usage. Not 100% accurate — use as a signal alongside other checks. Privacy: all analysis runs in your browser, no text uploaded.
