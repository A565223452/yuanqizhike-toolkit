How to Use

  1. Enter Roof length and width (footprint).
  2. Select Roof pitch (affects drainage area).
  3. Choose Max rainfall intensity for your area.
  4. Set Number of downspouts and gutter sides.
  5. Click Calculate.

5-inch K-style gutters handle most homes. Use 6-inch for larger roofs or steep pitches. Always slope gutters 1/4 inch per 10 ft toward downspouts.
