How to Use

  1. Choose a prayer type: morning, evening, gratitude, healing, guidance, protection, strength, peace, family, or meal blessing.
  2. Select tradition: Christian or General/Interfaith.
  3. Optionally enter a name to personalize the prayer.
  4. Optionally enter a specific intention (e.g. for healing of a loved one).
  5. Click Generate Prayer and copy as needed.

Prayers are randomly selected from a curated collection. Click Generate again for a different prayer. You can copy and share prayers freely.
