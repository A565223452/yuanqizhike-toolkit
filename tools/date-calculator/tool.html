How to Use

  1. Choose Difference between 2 dates or Add/Subtract from date.
  2. For difference: enter Start and End dates to see days/weeks/months/years.
  3. For add/subtract: enter base date, operation (add/subtract), value, and unit (days/weeks/months/years).
  4. Click Calculate to see the result.
