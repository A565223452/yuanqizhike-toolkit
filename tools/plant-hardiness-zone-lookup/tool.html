How to Use

  1. Choose lookup method: ZIP code or minimum temperature.
  2. For ZIP: enter 5-digit US ZIP code.
  3. For temp: enter average annual minimum temperature.
  4. Click Find Zone for plant recommendations.

USDA zones based on 30-year average minimum winter temperature. Plants rated for your zone or colder zones will typically survive. Local conditions may vary by 1 zone.
