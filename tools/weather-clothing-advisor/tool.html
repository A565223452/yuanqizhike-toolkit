How to Use

  1. Enter temperature and feels-like.
  2. Set humidity, wind, conditions.
  3. Choose your activity type.
  4. Get outfit recommendations.

Use "feels like" temperature, not actual. Layer system: base (moisture), mid (insulation), shell (weather). Cotton absorbs moisture—use synthetics or wool for active use. Cover head, hands, feet in cold. Activity warms you 15-20°F. Sun protection important regardless of temperature.
