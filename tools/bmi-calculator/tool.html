How to Use

  1. Choose unit system: Metric (cm, kg) or Imperial (ft/in, lbs).
  2. Enter your height and weight.
  3. Optionally enter your age and gender for ideal weight calculation.
  4. Click Calculate BMI to see your BMI, category, and healthy weight range.

BMI = weight(kg) / height(m)². Healthy range is 18.5-24.9. BMI may overestimate body fat in athletes and underestimate in older adults with muscle loss.
