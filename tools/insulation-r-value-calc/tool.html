How to Use

  1. Select Layer 1 material and thickness.
  2. Optionally add a Layer 2 for combined insulation.
  3. Choose Application (attic, wall, floor) and Climate zone.
  4. Click Calculate R-value.
  5. See total R-value vs recommended for your climate.

Higher R = better insulation. Closed-cell spray foam has highest R/inch but costs most. Air sealing before insulation is critical - even small gaps cut R-value significantly.
