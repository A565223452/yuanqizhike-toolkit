How to Use

  1. Enter your current mileage and select unit (miles or km).
  2. Enter vehicle age and annual mileage.
  3. Select vehicle type (gas, diesel, hybrid, electric).
  4. Choose driving conditions (normal or severe).
  5. Click Generate Schedule for maintenance plan.

Severe conditions (short trips, extreme temperatures, dusty roads, heavy traffic) require more frequent service. Electric vehicles have fewer maintenance items (no oil changes, spark plugs, etc.) but still need tire rotation, brake service, and battery checks.
