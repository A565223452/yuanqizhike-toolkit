How to Use

  1. Select ingredient (density varies).
  2. Enter amount and select unit.
  3. Click Convert.

Weight varies by ingredient: 1 cup flour = 120g, 1 cup sugar = 200g, 1 cup butter = 227g. Professional bakers use weight (grams) for accuracy. Spoon & level flour (don't scoop). Pack brown sugar. 1 cup = 16 tbsp = 48 tsp. 1 oz = 28.35g.
