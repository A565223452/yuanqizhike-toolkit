How to Use

  1. Select the Meat type you are smoking.
  2. Enter the Weight in pounds.
  3. Choose your Smoker temperature (225F is classic low & slow).
  4. Select Doneness target.
  5. Click Calculate BBQ Time for estimated time, timeline, and wood pairing.

Estimates account for the "stall" (evaporative cooling at 150-170F) on brisket and pork butt. Always verify with a meat thermometer - times can vary 25% based on weather, equipment, and meat thickness.
