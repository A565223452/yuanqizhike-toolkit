How to Use

  1. Enter your pet weight and select unit.
  2. Choose pet type (dog or cat).
  3. Select the medication from the list.
  4. Click Calculate Dose for weight-based dosage.
Note: NEVER give these to pets (TOXIC): — Tylenol (acetaminophen) - deadly to cats, dangerous to dogs — Advil/Motrin (ibuprofen) - causes stomach ulcers, kidney failure — Aleve (naproxen) - highly toxic — Any cold/sinus medication with pseudoephedrine — Xylitol (in some melatonin gummies)

This calculator is for informational purposes only and is NOT a substitute for veterinary advice. Always verify dosages with your vet before administering any medication.
