How to Use

  1. Enter recipient and your name.
  2. Choose the occasion (interview, gift, wedding, etc).
  3. Specify what they did/gave in detail.
  4. Add the impact - how it affected you.
  5. Pick a tone (warm, formal, casual).
  6. Click Generate then copy or download.

Send thank you notes promptly - within 24 hours for interviews, 1-2 weeks for gifts. Specific details make notes feel genuine, not generic.
