How to Use

  1. Add courses with credits and grades.
  2. Click + Add Course for more.
  3. Optional: enter previous GPA for cumulative.
  4. Click Calculate GPA.

4.0 scale: A=4.0, A-=3.7, B+=3.3, B=3.0, etc. Weighted by credit hours. Cumulative GPA combines all semesters. Honors: Summa Cum Laude ≥3.9, Magna ≥3.7, Cum Laude ≥3.5. Most schools require ≥2.0 to graduate.
