How to Use

  1. Set your aperture, shutter speed, and ISO.
  2. See the resulting Exposure Value (EV).
  3. View equivalent exposures at different apertures.
  4. Use equivalents to achieve different depth of field or motion blur.

EV combines aperture, shutter, and ISO into one number. Lower EV = brighter scene (needs less exposure). Each "stop" doubles or halves the light. Wide aperture (f/1.4) = shallow depth of field. Fast shutter (1/4000) = freeze motion.
