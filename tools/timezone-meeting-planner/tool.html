How to Use

  1. Add participant timezones from the dropdown.
  2. Set working hours and meeting duration.
  3. Click Find best times to see top 5 options.
  4. Review the 24-hour timeline for visual overlap.

Uses UTC offsets (no DST adjustment). For precise scheduling, verify with each participant. Best meeting times typically fall in overlap of all working hours.
