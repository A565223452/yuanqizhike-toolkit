How to Use

  1. Enter current weather conditions.
  2. Choose time of day and season.
  3. See mosquito activity level.
  4. Get repellent recommendations.

Mosquitoes are most active at dawn/dusk, 75-85°F, high humidity, low wind. Recent rain increases breeding. Wind >10mph reduces activity. Use DEET 20-30% for typical protection, 50%+ for high-risk areas. Eliminate standing water around home.
