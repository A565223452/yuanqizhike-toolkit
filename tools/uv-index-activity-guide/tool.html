How to Use

  1. Enter the UV Index (check your local weather report).
  2. Select your Skin type (Fitzpatrick scale 1-6).
  3. Enter planned Time outside (minutes) and SPF sunscreen.
  4. Click Assess risk.
  5. Review burn time, protection advice, and recommended activities.

UV Index 0-2 = Low, 3-5 = Moderate, 6-7 = High, 8-10 = Very High, 11+ = Extreme. Always reapply sunscreen every 2 hours or after swimming.
