How to Use

  1. Enter your desired annual income (after-tax take-home).
  2. Add business expenses (software, equipment, etc.).
  3. Set billable hours per week (realistically 20-30).
  4. Account for vacation weeks, tax rate, and profit margin.
  5. See your target hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly rates.

Only ~60% of a freelancer's time is billable. The rest goes to admin, sales, and training. Plan for at least 25% profit margin to reinvest in your business.
