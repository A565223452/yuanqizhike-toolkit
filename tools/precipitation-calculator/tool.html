How to Use

  1. Mode 1: Calculate rainwater catchment volume.
  2. Mode 2: Convert snow depth to water equivalent.
  3. Mode 3: Calculate rainfall rate (intensity).

1 mm rain on 1 m² = 1 liter water. Snow ratios: fresh 10:1, average 8:1, packed 5:1, wet 3:1. Rainfall intensity: light <2.5 mm/hr, moderate 2.5-7.5, heavy 7.5-15, very heavy 15-30, extreme >30 mm/hr. Roof catchment can provide significant water supply.
