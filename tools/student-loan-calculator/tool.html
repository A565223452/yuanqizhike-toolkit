How to Use

  1. Select the loan type (auto-fills current federal rates).
  2. Enter the loan amount.
  3. Adjust the interest rate if needed.
  4. Choose the repayment term (5-25 years).
  5. Enter extra monthly payment to see savings.
  6. Expand the schedule to see month-by-month breakdown.

US federal loan rates shown for 2024-25 academic year. Private loans vary widely. Consult your loan servicer for exact terms. This calculator provides estimates only.
