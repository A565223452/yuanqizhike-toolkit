How to Use

  1. Select gender and height unit.
  2. Enter height, weight, age.
  3. Optional: choose frame size.
  4. Click Calculate.

BMI = weight(kg) / height(m)². Categories: <18.5 underweight, 18.5-24.9 normal, 25-29.9 overweight, -0 obese. IBW formulas (Devine, Hamwi, Robinson, Miller) estimate ideal weight from height. Limitations: BMI doesn\'t distinguish muscle from fat. Athletes may have high BMI but low body fat. Asians: risk starts at BMI ~ 23.
